# Metal 4: An overview
## Summary
This article summarizes Apple’s announced Metal 4 graphics API direction and highlights major capabilities for macOS/iOS-era platforms. It frames Metal 4 as an Apple Silicon–first evolution with stronger graphics and compute features, especially around ray tracing and ML-aware rendering paths. The piece is written as an early overview of publicly available information.

## Key Points
- Metal 4 is announced in the macOS Tahoe / iOS 26 timeframe and targets newer Apple Silicon-class hardware.
- New MetalFX features include frame interpolation and denoising for ray/path tracing pipelines.
- Temporal upscaling support is highlighted for dynamic resolution handling.
- MTLTensor is introduced as a tensor-focused data structure for GPU ML workloads.
- The article notes ongoing convergence between Apple ML tooling and broader GPU ecosystems.
