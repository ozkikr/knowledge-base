# Anthony Bourdain's Lost Li.st's

## Summary
A partial archive of approximately 30 lists Anthony Bourdain published on the defunct li.st platform around 2015, recovered from the Internet Archive. Includes playlists, food recommendations, and personal musings. Most missing lists have since been recovered by other archivists.

## Key Points
- ~30 lists originally published on li.st circa 2015
- Recovered from Internet Archive's Wayback Machine
- Includes 80s playlists, travel recommendations, cultural commentary
- Community effort helped recover most missing lists
- Links to additional recoveries by Sandy Uraz and mirandrom
