# Nomos-1: Mathematical Reasoning Model by NousResearch

## Summary
Nomos-1 is a specialization of Qwen3-30B-A3B-Thinking for mathematical problem-solving and proof-writing in natural language. Trained in collaboration with Hillclimb AI, it scores 87/120 on Putnam 2025 (vs 24/120 for the base model) when used with the Nomos Reasoning Harness.
