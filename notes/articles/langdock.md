# Langdock — Enterprise AI Adoption Platform

Platform for rolling out AI across an entire organization in a safe and flexible way.

## Summary

Langdock is an enterprise AI adoption platform that enables organizations to deploy AI company-wide with security, cost control, and flexibility. It serves as an all-in-one platform for both simple daily AI usage by business users and advanced use cases built by technical teams.

## Key Points

- **Enterprise-scale adoption**: 33,000+ MAU at some customers (e.g., referenced case study)
- **All-in-one platform**: simple for business users, ready for advanced use cases
- Trusted by 4,000+ companies
- Focus on controlled, secure, cost-effective AI environment
- Step-by-step organizational AI adoption guidance
- Positioned as "the essential AI stack for your company"
