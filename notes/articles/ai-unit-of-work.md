# The Quality of AI-Assisted Software Depends on Unit of Work Management

## Summary
The craft of AI-assisted software creation is substantially about correctly managing units of work. The article argues that the major bottleneck isn't model intelligence but providing correct context, and that working on small, concrete chunks is key to getting quality results from AI coding tools.
