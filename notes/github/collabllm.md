# CollabLLM: From Passive Responders to Active Collaborators

## Summary
CollabLLM transforms language models from passive responders to active collaborators in multi-turn conversations. Winner of ICML 2025 Outstanding Paper Award. Provides a framework for computing multiturn-aware rewards and training collaborative LLMs via SFT/DPO.

## Key Points
- ICML 2025 Outstanding Paper Award
- Introduces Multiturn-aware Rewards (MRs) for evaluating conversation quality
- Supports SFT and DPO training to maximize collaborative behavior
- Installable via pip, supports distributed training with DeepSpeed
- Integrates with verl for RL training
