# Product of Experts with LLMs: ARC Paper

## Summary
Code for the ICML 2025 paper achieving 71.6% on the public ARC-AGI evaluation set using Product of Experts with LLMs. Combines fine-tuned language models with algorithmic sampling strategies and test-time training for state-of-the-art ARC performance.

## Key Points
- Achieves 71.6% (286.5/400 tasks) on public ARC-AGI eval set
- Two models released: NeMo-Minitron-8B (71.6%) and Llama-3.2-3B (61.4%)
- Uses Product of Experts approach with multiple grid perspectives
- Includes test-time training for improved performance
- Published at ICML 2025
