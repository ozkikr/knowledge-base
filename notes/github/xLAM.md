# xLAM - Large Action Models

## Summary
A family of Large Action Models by Salesforce AI Research designed to empower AI agent systems. Includes models, training framework (ActionStudio), and benchmarks for function-calling and tool use. xLAM-2-fc-r achieved #1 on the BFCL Leaderboard.

## Key Points
- Family of models specifically trained for action/tool-calling tasks
- ActionStudio framework for training action models (accepted at EMNLP 2025)
- Top-1 performance on Berkeley Function Calling Leaderboard (BFCL)
- Includes APIGen-MT-5k dataset for multi-turn function-calling
- Paper, training code, and models all open-sourced
